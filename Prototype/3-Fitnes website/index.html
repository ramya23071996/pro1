FAQ FOR REVIEWS

1.) How does the fitness tracker work?

Our fitness tracker records your daily steps, calories buried,heart rate,
and workout activity using your phone or wearable device sensors.

2.) IS my health data safe and private?

Yes!:We use end-to-end encryption and never share your personal health
data without your consent. You have full control over what you choose to track and store.

3.) Does the app include a meal or diet planner?

Yes,the app includes a built-in meal planner and food tracker. You can
set daily calories goals,log meals,and get healthy eating tips based on your fitness goals.
